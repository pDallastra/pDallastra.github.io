Preston Girls fall in overtime to Sugar-Salem

by newbreaks.com

An impressive comeback gave Preston a fighting chance against 3A power Sugar-Salem, but the Diggers regrouped just enough in overtime. The Indians trailed by 15 points heading into the fourth quarter, but outscored the Diggers 16-1 to force overtime. Visiting Sugar-Salem scored the winning points with less than 25 seconds remaining in OT to hold off Preston, 63-62, on Wednesday afternoon.
